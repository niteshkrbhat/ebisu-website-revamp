About Ebisu Finance

Ebisu Finance’s codeless platform offers a complete suite of token management tools, for both projects as well as investors. It’s a de-centralised platform, supporting multiple chains, where projects can create new tokens, lock liquidity and schedule automated token vesting whereas investors can check locked liquidity, vesting schedule and also access our marketplace to trade unvested tokens.





For Projects

Our no-code platform offer multiple cross chain tools including token generation, automated vesting, cross-chain bridging, staking, airdrops, and more.All the tools at Ebisu Fiance can be used by anyone without coding experience



For Investors

Ebisu Finance provide transparent information of locked tokens and liquidity, along with vesting schedule and staking.Ebisu Finance's unique marketplace allows investors to trade locked tokens and earn profit before vesting
